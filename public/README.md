# Music Files for Background Music

To add background music to your Valentine's Surprise:

1. **Add your music file here**: Place your romantic background music file in this `public` folder
2. **Name it**: `romantic-music.mp3` (or update the filename in `BackgroundMusic.tsx`)
3. **Recommended formats**: MP3, WAV, or OGG
4. **File size**: Keep it under 5MB for faster loading

## Music Suggestions:
- Soft instrumental piano music
- Gentle acoustic guitar
- Classical romantic pieces
- Soft jazz instrumentals
- Ambient romantic music

## How to use:
1. Place your music file as `romantic-music.mp3` in this folder
2. The music player will automatically detect it
3. Users can control volume and play/pause from the music button in the bottom-right corner

Note: Make sure you have the rights to use any music you add!
